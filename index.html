About Kedi Trail Adventures

A Kenyan house of quiet luxury and real adventure.

For over a decade we've been building journeys the way our grandparents built stories — slowly, generously, and always with the guest in mind. Every itinerary is drawn by hand by a Nairobi-based designer who has slept in every camp we recommend.

We work only with lodges and guides that treat the land and its people with the same care we do. Small, focused, and always in season.
